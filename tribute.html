DIEGO MARADONA

1960 - 2020

When God decides its time, I guess he'll come for us.

About

  • 1960 - Born on 30 October 1960, in the Argentine city of Lanus.
  • 1974 - He signed with Argentinos Juniors at age 14.
  • 1976 - He had his professional debut for Argentinos Juniors.
  • 1979- He led the under-20 team to a Junior World Cup championship.
  • 1981 - He moved to Boca Juniors and immediately helped them gain the championship.
  • 1982 - Spanish giants, Barcelona, signed him for a world record fee of £5 million.
  • 1983 - He won the Spanish Cup with Barcelona.
  • 1984 - He moved to Italy, playing for SSC Napoli.
  • 1986 - Maradona captained the Argentine national team to victory in the 1986 World Cup in Mexico
  • 1987 - With Maradona the team won the league title and cup.
  • 1990 - He won the league title again.
  • 1991 - He was arrested in Argentina for cocaine possession and received a 15-month suspension from playing football.
  • 1995 - He returned to Boca Juniors.
  • 2000 - He won the FIFA Player of the Century award.
  • 2020 - On 2 November 2020, Maradona was admitted to a hospital in La Plata.
  • 2020- On 25 November 2020, the Legend, Maradona, died after he suffered a heart attack.

To read more about Diego Maradona, go to his page at wikipedia.
